Question:
Display month of sale and number of sales done in that month, sorted in descending order of sales.

Oracle SQL Query:
SELECT TO_CHAR(Sldate, 'FMMonth') AS MONTH,
       COUNT(Saleid) AS NUMBER_SALE
FROM Sale
GROUP BY TO_CHAR(Sldate, 'FMMonth'),
         TO_CHAR(Sldate, 'MM')
ORDER BY COUNT(Saleid) DESC;
