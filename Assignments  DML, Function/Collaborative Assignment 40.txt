Question:

Display the itemtype and classification of items. The classification is:

Price between 0 and 499 → 'Cheap'
Price between 500 and 1999 → 'Affordable'
Price between 2000 and 4999 → 'Expensive'
Price >= 5000 → 'Very Expensive'

Display unique rows sorted by Itemtype and Classification in ascending order.

Query:

SELECT DISTINCT Itemtype,
       CASE
           WHEN Price BETWEEN 0 AND 499 THEN 'Cheap'
           WHEN Price BETWEEN 500 AND 1999 THEN 'Affordable'
           WHEN Price BETWEEN 2000 AND 4999 THEN 'Expensive'
           WHEN Price >= 5000 THEN 'Very Expensive'
       END AS Classification
FROM Item
ORDER BY Itemtype ASC, Classification ASC;
