Question:

A discount of 25.5% is being offered on all FMCG items. Display Itemcode, existing unit price as "Old Price", and discounted price as "New Price". Round off the discounted price to 2 decimal places.

Query:

SELECT Itemcode,
       Price AS "Old Price",
       ROUND(Price * (1 - 25.5/100), 2) AS "New Price"
FROM Item
WHERE Itemtype = 'FMCG';
