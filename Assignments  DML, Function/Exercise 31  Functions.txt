Question:

A discount of 22.5% is being offered on all Sports category items. Display Prodid, Pdesc, existing unit price as "Old_Price", and discounted price as "New_Price". Round off the discounted price to two decimal places.

Query:

SELECT Prodid,
       Pdesc,
       Price AS "Old_Price",
       ROUND(Price * 0.775, 2) AS "New_Price"
FROM Product
WHERE Category = 'Sports';
