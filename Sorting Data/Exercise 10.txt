Display Product ID, Category and Discount of the products which belong to the category 'Sports' or 'Apparel' in ascending order of Category and Discount.

Table Structure:

ColumnName	DataType	Description
Prodid	NUMBER	Unique ID of the product
Pdesc	VARCHAR2	Description of the product
Price	NUMBER	Price of the product
Category	VARCHAR2	Category of the product
Discount	NUMBER	Discount percentage of the product

Query :

SELECT Prodid, Category, Discount
FROM Product
WHERE Category IN ('Sports', 'Apparel')
ORDER BY Category ASC, Discount ASC;


You can also write the condition as:

WHERE Category = 'Sports' OR Category = 'Apparel'
